About :

After being inspired by the “Theme of Aldia” from Dark Souls II Scholar of the first Sin by Motoi Sakuraba and Yuka Kitamu; I came back on piano arrangement after a while.
I tried to play a lot with notes outside the scale, without playing too much on dissonant chords. It was quite fun to study, but I think I'm still too restricted when it comes to unusual chords.
