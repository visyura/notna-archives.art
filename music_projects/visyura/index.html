Visyura is the birth the will of mine to achieve things I'be proud of with music production. I never did anything conclusive of finished or polished before and this was the proof I could show things to other people and get things done. It's funny because it initiated as a project that was aimed to make numbers - but obviously, I put so much of myself in and that became the focus of it.

Grown from internet music Inspirations of this project are heavily based on 2020 Form era artists such as Glances, Intrex, Defsharp. Since I stopped producing for a year, I also stopped digging and had these compitlations downloaded in my phone and they really stuck in me. I'm really happy to what this project became, I met a lot of people online and in person that helped me to improve so much and most importantly : made me feel less alone. Met friends, grew confidence and self-esteem,

By the years, I'm not so much into this kind of production and everything that comes along today but who knows.
